About Us


Origins

Stoigela is a company that was born out of necessity, has found its inspiration in historical data, its development rooted in curiosity, and its formulation grounded in scientific evidence. This culminated in the development of Stoigela’s premium Zoilevium collection.

  • Born out of necessity: In 2012 the founders of Stoigela took note of the unspoken pandemic that was already wreaking havoc in the world and was being touted as one of the biggest threats to human health in future, namely antimicrobial resistance (AMR). The rise of superbugs and the fact that antibiotics are increasingly losing the fight against resistant pathogens, have prompted the World Health Organization to describe AMR as one of the top public health and development threats globally. With the world urgently searching for a solution to the AMR threat, Stoigela decided to research alternative solutions to the problem.
  • Inspired by historical data: Research into alternative antimicrobial agents revealed that trace minerals have been a common antimicrobial agent and source of medicinal applications since ancient times. This reality has for example convinced Professor Michael G. Schmidt, a professor of microbiology and immunology at the Medical University of South Carolina, to refer to Copper as “truly a gift from Mother Nature, in that the human race has been using it for over eight millennia." From historical records, it became evident that the use of trace minerals as antimicrobials is nothing new. Unfortunately, trace minerals fell out of favour with the discovery of antibiotics and antiseptics.
  • Development rooted in curiosity: Based on the historical evidence and driven by a curious demeanour, the research turned towards trace minerals as a next-generation metallo-antibiotic and well-being contributor. In a certain sense Newton's third law of motion, namely Action and Reaction, inspired the development of the Zoilevium product range. Newton’s law represents a certain symmetry in nature. The law states that for every force in nature, there is an equal and opposite force (or counterforce). The argument simply was that if this is true for all motion in the universe, maybe it can apply to living things too. Maybe nature created pathogens but simultaneously created the antidote as well. In our opinion that antidote is trace minerals and vitamins. 
  • Formulations grounded in scientific evidence: After 12 years of experimenting with various combinations and blending processes and over 5,000 laboratory efficacy tests, the magical formulation and blending processes were discovered and refined. With numerous external scientific and academical published and peer-reviewed research as backing, we are more than confident in the quality and efficacy of our Zoilevium Trace Mineral Product Range. The academical research shows that the use of metal-based products as an antimicrobial, is not new science, it is only forgotten science. Maybe paraphrasing Sir Arthur Conan Doyle’s Sherlock Holmes might be in order - When you have eliminated the impossible (trying over and over to create new antibiotics that just aren’t working), whatever remains (a new-old metal-based approach), however improbable, must be the truth.
