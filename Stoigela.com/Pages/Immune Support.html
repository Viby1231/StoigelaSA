Zoilevium Immune Support

Zoilevium Immune Support assists the body in fortifying one's immune system. An optimized immune system is crucial for maintaining overall health, as it protects the body from infections, reduces the risk of chronic diseases, and ensures that the body's defences are strong enough to pre-emptively fight off illnesses effectively. Therefore, preventing illness, before curing illness, becomes necessary. A robust immune system helps in faster recovery, better resilience against stress, and overall improved well-being.

The modes of action that Zoilevium Immune Support relies on, to assist with strengthening the immune system, are found in the inherently acknowledged properties of the trace minerals it comprises. The benefit of minerals, as supported by SAHPRA in South Africa:

  • Zinc: Helps strengthen the immune system, promotes digestive health, has antibacterial properties, contributes to normal cognitive function, contributes to normal DNA synthesis, helps with macronutrient metabolism, helps maintain normal testosterone levels in the blood, helps protect cells from oxidative stress, assists in the maintenance of good health.

  • Magnesium: Has anti-inflammatory properties, contributes to tissue formation, contributes to normal energy-yielding metabolism, contributes to the normal functioning of the nervous system, helps with the reduction of tiredness and fatigue, helps maintain normal muscle function, contributes to normal psychological function, assists in the maintenance of good health.

  • Copper: Has anti-inflammatory properties, promotes formation of red blood cells, has antibacterial properties, protects cells from damage and oxidative stress, contributes to normal energy-yielding metabolism, contributes to the normal function of the immune system, contributes to normal skin and hair pigmentation.

  • Potassium: Contributes to normal function of the nervous system, contributes to normal muscle function, contributes to the maintenance of normal blood pressure.

  • Citric acid: Assists in absorbing nutrients.

​​ All of these properties may contribute to promoting the reinforcement of the body’s natural immune system.
