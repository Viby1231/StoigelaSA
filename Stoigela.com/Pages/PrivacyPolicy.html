Privacy Policy

Introduction

We are committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors and users. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal information when you use our website www.stoigela.com and services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: We collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you:

  • Create an account or sign up for a newsletter
  • Make a purchase or place an order
  • Contact us through our website or customer support

Non-Personal Information: We collect non-personal information such as:

  • Browser type and device type
  • Operating system and platform
  • IP address and location
  • Website usage and browsing history

How We Use Your Information

Personal Information: We use personal information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Process and fulfil orders and transactions
  • Send newsletters and marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services

Non-Personal Information: We use non-personal information to:

  • Analyse website usage and improve user experience
  • Enhance our marketing efforts
  • Identify and prevent fraud and security risks

Information Sharing

Third-Party Service Providers: We share personal information with third-party service providers who assist us with:

  • Order fulfilment and shipping
  • Payment processing
  • Customer support

Your data will never be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Legal Requirements: We disclose personal information when required by law or to protect our rights and property.

Data Security

  • Encryption: We use industry-standard encryption to protect personal information in transit.
  • Firewalls and Access Controls: We have implemented firewalls and access controls to protect personal information from unauthorized access.

Your Rights

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to access and correct your personal information.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information.
  • Object to Processing: You have the right to object to processing of your personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website or sending an email.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@stoigela.com.

Effective Date

This privacy policy is effective as of 2024/04/27.
