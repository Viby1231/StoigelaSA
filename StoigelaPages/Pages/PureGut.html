Zoilevium PureGut

Zoilevium PureGut is a mineral-based, inorganic, antimicrobial compound. The modes of action that it relies on, to assist with gut-related problems and conditions like IBS, are found in the inherently acknowledged properties of the trace minerals it comprises. The benefits of minerals, as supported by SAHPRA in South Africa:

  • Copper: It has anti-inflammatory properties, promotes the formation of red blood cells, helps with the absorption of iron in the body, it has strong antibacterial properties, it protects cells from damage and helps to produce and repair connective tissue.

  • Zinc: Supports the body’s immune function, it promotes digestive health, it has antibacterial properties, it helps to normalise macro-nutrient metabolism, it supports connective tissue formation, it also helps the body to metabolise carbohydrates, fats and proteins.

  • Magnesium: It has anti-inflammatory properties, it helps metabolise carbohydrates, fats and proteins and contributes to tissue formation.

​​ All of these properties may contribute to counteracting the common symptoms of an irritated gastrointestinal tract.
